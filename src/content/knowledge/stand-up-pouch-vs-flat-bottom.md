When choosing flexible packaging, many brands hesitate between **stand-up pouches** and **flat bottom pouches**.  
Both are popular, but they serve different needs.

## Stand-Up Pouch

Stand-up pouches are widely used for snacks, gummies, powders, and supplements.

**Key features:**
- Lower cost
- Lightweight
- Suitable for small to medium volumes

## Flat Bottom Pouch

Flat bottom pouches (also known as box pouches) offer a premium look.

**Key features:**
- Strong shelf presence
- More printable area
- Suitable for higher-end brands

## Which One Should You Choose?

If cost efficiency is your priority, stand-up pouches work well.  
If branding and shelf impact matter more, flat bottom pouches are a better choice.
